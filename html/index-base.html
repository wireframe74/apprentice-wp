Your partner in productivity

Ai Group Apprentice and Trainee Centre is part of a national system with over 35,000 apprentices and trainees in wide and varied trades and employment.

We place apprentices and trainees with host organisations across all states and territories, and are a proud partner to more than 200 host organisations including some of Australia’s leading organisations, many of whom are household names. Our success is based on rigorous selection of capable, willing and determined people, an industry leading apprentice management system and award winning service.

Ai Group Apprentice and Trainee Centre is an efficient alternative to directly employing apprentices and trainees. We pride ourselves on going beyond what is expected of apprentice management services. Involvement in government bodies and Industry Training Boards ensures we are at the forefront of skills focused decision making and policy development.

Ai Group Apprentice and Trainee Centre is proudly sponsored by Klein Tools.
